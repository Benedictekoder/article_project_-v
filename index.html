The Amanita Family of Mushrooms

Amanitaceae

Facts

The Amanita family consists of over 600 species.

Some are deadly poisonous if eaten, and others are edible.

Amanitas can be found across every continent except north and southpole.

Amanita Muscaria

With its destinct red colouration and white dots, this is perhaps the most famous member of the Amanita family. Known as a toadstool from fairytales and the mushroom that makes Super Mario enlarged. Amanita Muscaria is poisonous and should never be eaten raw. Comes in several subspecies, like the yellow/peachyish Amanita Percensis
